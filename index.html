The Volta River Authority exists to power economies and raise the living standards of the people of Ghana and West Africa

Our Mission

To generate, transmit and supply electricity and related services in a reliable, safe and environmentally friendly manner to add economic, financial and social values to the lives of our stakeholders.

Our Vision

To be a leading power utility in Africa, providing reliable, affordable and sustainable energy solutions while contributing to economic development and environmental stewardship.

Our Commitment

Committed to sustainable energy development, environmental protection, and powering Ghana's industrial growth through innovative and reliable electricity generation and transmission services.
